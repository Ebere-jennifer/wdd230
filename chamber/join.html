Membership Level Benefits

NP Membership (free)

The NP Membership is designed for non-profit organizations to help them grow and connect within the community without any fees.

Benefits for NP Membership

  • No fee for non-profit organizations
  • Access to special events, basic training, general support.

Bronze Membership ($15)

The Bronze Membership offers greater visibility and networking opportunities to help small businesses increase their reach and connections.

Benefits for Bronze Membership

  • Increased visibility on the chamber's website
  • Access to networking events
  • Discounted rates for chamber-sponsored workshops

Silver Membership ($25)

The Silver Membership enjoy enhanced benefits, including priority event access and exclusive advertising opportunities, aimed at medium-sized businesses.

Benefits for Silver Membership

  • All benefits of Bronze Membership
  • Priority access to chamber-sponsored events
  • Exclusive advertising opportunities
  • Access to specialized training programs

Gold Membership ($50)

The Gold Membership receive premium benefits with maximum exposure and leadership opportunities within the chamber community.

Benefits for Gold Membership

  • All benefits of Silver Membership
  • Featured placement on the chamber's website
  • Recognition at chamber events
  • Opportunity to lead chamber committees
  • Access to personal business consultations
