Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help provide information about economic development and community events that support local commerce, join the chamber and as well provide information regarding companies belonging to the chamber.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Lagos Chamber of Commerce?

The Lagos Chamber of Commerce is an organization that represents the business community in Lagos, Nigeria, providing resources, networking opportunities, and advocacy for local businesses.

How can I join the Lagos Chamber of Commerce?

You can join the Lagos Chamber of Commerce by visiting our Join Page and filling out the membership application form.

What benefits do members receive?

Members receive access to exclusive networking events, business resources, advocacy services, and more.

Are there any events organized by the Lagos Chamber of Commerce?

Yes, we organize various events including business expos, networking events, workshops, and seminars. You can check our event calendar for upcoming events.

How can I contact the Lagos Chamber of Commerce?

You can contact us via email at info@lagoschamber.com or call us at +234 123 4567. Our office is open from Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.
